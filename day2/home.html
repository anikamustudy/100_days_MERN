Welcome to My Homepage

Hello, I’m Anika Sapkota a creative learner and tech enthusiast who loves combining coding and art to build meaningful digital experiences.

I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern web technologies to create clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites. For me, coding is not just about logic; it’s also a form of creativity where ideas turn into functional designs.

Alongside technology, I have a deep interest in arts and creative expression. Art inspires my design thinking, improves my problem-solving approach, and helps me create work that feels both functional and human.

I believe in continuous learning, creativity, and supporting local value. Through this platform, I share my journey in technology, art, and meaningful creations.
